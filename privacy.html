Overview

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

Information we collect

  • Contact details (such as name, email, phone number)
  • Business information you submit (such as industry, location, goals)
  • Booking and enquiry details you provide
  • Website usage data (such as pages visited and device/browser information)

How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and book calls
  • To deliver and improve our services
  • To communicate updates and important information
  • To improve website performance and user experience

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as scheduling, analytics, or communication tools) only to operate our business.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, but we work hard to keep data protected.

Your choices

  • You can request access, updates, or deletion of your information.
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time.
  • You can manage cookies in your browser settings.
